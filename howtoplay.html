How to play

Step 1: The concept of 'Coin Fusion' is a fantasy trading experience with real-time market data. Dive into the world of cryptocurrencies, make strategic decisions, analyze trends. Basically it is a fusion of Crypto currency and Fantasy League of gaming and trade.

Read More

Step 2: Each user will start out with 500 coins and they must make a prediction on whether the market will gain profit or not. Outcomes will be displayed on the leaderboard as per your prediction. .

Read More

Step 3: If the player's prediction comes true, the coins will be doubled; if not, the player will forfeit the coins they initially spent. .

Read More

Step 4: If the game is won the amount will be credited to the wallet and user can use those amount in the next prediction. .
